Who I Am

I'm Erin, a third-year Computer Engineering student at De La Salle University from Angeles City, Pampanga. I originally set out to study Computer Science, but ended up in CpE — and honestly, it's grown on me. It's pushed me in ways I didn't expect, even if I'm still figuring out which corner of the field I want to eventually land in.

Outside of school, I spend a lot of time commuting around Manila just to see where things lead — different neighborhoods, different food, different energy. I also watch movies alone at the cinema, cook, read, and do sudoku. I like my own company, and I like observing things.

Where I'm Headed

I'm pursuing a minor in Financial Risk and Analytics — less because I've fully merged it with my engineering work, and more because I find value in understanding how data informs decisions, especially financial ones.
